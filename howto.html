How to Use Plane Identifier AR

  1. Open the app and grant camera permissions.
  2. Point your phone’s camera to the sky to activate AeroScan AR.
  3. Wait a moment for the app to detect and identify planes.
  4. Tap on identified planes for detailed info.
  5. Switch to Map Mode to see planes in your area on the map.
