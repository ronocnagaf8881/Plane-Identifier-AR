About AeroScan

AeroScan is the core scanning technology inside Plane Identifier AR. It uses augmented reality (AR) to scan the sky and recognize aircraft models instantly.

By combining camera input and a comprehensive aircraft database, AeroScan delivers real-time plane identification and details.
